About Us

Restaurant that makes People happy

At the point when "Hauz" organizer Demitrio ventured off the shorelines of El Nido following multi-day of surfing with his Navy pals in the mid-60s, he did what worked out easily for him. He went hunting down a delicious barbecues .Today, Hauz’s still is consistent with Demitrio’s unique experience and his unique vision. Also, however, our menu has developed to incorporate plates of meats, appetizers, and delicate desserts.
